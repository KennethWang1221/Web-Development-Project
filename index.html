The FIFA World Cup, the FIFA World Cup, is the world’s highest honor, highest specification, highest gold content, and highest visibility football game. It is also known as the two top sports events in the world with the Olympic Games, and even has a broadcast coverage of more than The world's largest sports event for the Olympics.

The World Cup is the most coveted sacred glory in the football field of all countries in the world. Which national football team can win the championship is the number one in the world, and the whole world will be crazy about it. Players who perform well in the World Cup will be regarded as national heroes in the history of the country, so it also represents the ultimate dream of every football player.

The World Cup is held every four years, and any FIFA member country (region) can send a team to register for this event.

The Brazilian national men's football team is currently the team that has won the most honors. It has won 5 World Cup championships. After 3 World Cups, it has permanently retained the former World Cup Remit Gold Cup. The current World Cup is the Hercules Cup. Germany won the cup for the first time in 1974 and has been used today, collectively referred to as the World Cup.
